## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a security vulnerability, please email us at [security@example.com](mailto:security@example.com). 

Please include as much detail as possible about the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it. We request that you do not publicly disclose the issue until we have had a chance to investigate and address it.

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 2 business days. You can expect updates at least every 7 days until the issue is resolved. If the vulnerability is accepted, we will work with you to coordinate a fix and public disclosure. If the vulnerability is declined, we will provide a clear explanation of our reasoning.
